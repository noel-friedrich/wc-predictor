Simple World Cup Predictor

The default strengths were assigned by a large language model (ChatGPT 5.5). You can see the exact prompt I used here. Of course, these may not reflect reality well, but are likely a better source than some arbitrary values.

This tool lets you simulate the knockout stage of the 2026 FIFA World Cup statistically. I also made a YouTube video to explain this tool. You can assign a strength value (from $0.1$ to $10.0$) to each team. In a given matchup, the probability that team $A$ beats team $B$ is $$\mathbb{P}(A\text{ beats } B) = \frac{\text{str}(A)}{\text{str}(A) + \text{str}(B)}$$

You can edit these strengths and see how the corresponding win probabilities change. Here is the bracket that would result if the stronger team beat the weaker team every time:

By playing around with the strengths, you may notice that the placement (e.g. which side you start on) heavily influences a team's chance of advancing; teams with lower strength may have a higher likelihood of winning than high-strength teams.
